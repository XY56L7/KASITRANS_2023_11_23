Who are We?

The company was founded by Péter Kasi and Róbert Kasi in 2004. We mostly operate with open trailers, and have multiple years of experience regarding oversized land shipments. Within our ever-expanding machine park, you can find hydraulic tipper trailer, split trailer, ramp mega trailer and vehicle transporters. These vehicles posess year-round oversized transportation permits in the following countries: Germany, Austria, Italy, Belgium, Switzerland, Luxemburg, Hungary, France, and Poland. Our transports are regularly maintained, and are well equipped to be able to travel these countries according to their regulations. Currently there are twenty four such vehicles operate from within our machine park, which are continuously outfited with the latest needs of our age, so we may live up to it's challanges.

HOW CAN WE HELP?

Oversized cargo transportation across the entire EU


With several years of experience in oversized cargo transportation, our fleet operates with annual permits in almost every country in the EU. We can obtain special permits for unique cases. We are capable of transporting long, wide, and high-dimensional special cargo.
